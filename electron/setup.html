What This Application Does

Org Health Agent connects to your Salesforce orgs via the Salesforce CLI, retrieves metadata and runtime data, and uses AI (Google Gemini) to analyze your org's health across 294 parameters in 11 categories.

Reports are generated locally and stored in a local database. Metadata summaries are sent to the configured AI model for analysis only.

What is Salesforce CLI?

Salesforce CLI (sf) is a command-line tool from Salesforce used to authenticate with orgs, retrieve metadata, and execute queries. This agent uses it to gather data about your org's health.

Download from: https://developer.salesforce.com/tools/salesforcecli
